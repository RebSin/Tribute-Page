Some Details Regarding Mr. Page:

  • He was born March 26, 1973
  • He is one of the co-founders of Google along with Sergey Brin
  • He was the chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc. (Google's parent company)
  • Forbes placed him 10th in the list "Billionaires 2019"
  • He is the co-inventor of PageRank, a well-known search ranking algorithm for Google, which he wrote with Brin
